Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Safe Wear System

How does the three-sensor verification prevent false alarms?

The system requires simultaneous anomalies from all three sensors (heart rate, sound, and voice recognition) before declaring an emergency. This multi-layered approach ensures that normal activities like exercise, loud environments, or casual conversation don't trigger false alerts.

What happens when the emergency button is pressed?

The manual emergency button bypasses all sensors and immediately sends an alert with your GPS location to pre-configured emergency contacts and services. This ensures help can be summoned even when you cannot speak or in quiet environments.

How long does the battery last?

The ESP32-based system is optimized for low power consumption, providing up to 24 hours of continuous monitoring on a single charge. The device also includes power-saving modes and low battery alerts.

Can I customize the emergency contacts?

Absolutely! The companion app allows you to set up multiple emergency contacts, including family members, friends, and local emergency services. You can prioritize contacts and set different alert types for each.

How accurate is the GPS tracking?

The integrated GPS module provides location accuracy within 3-5 meters under normal conditions. The system also supports assisted GPS for faster location acquisition and works with multiple satellite systems for improved reliability.
